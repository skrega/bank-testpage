Rewards Credit Card

The credit card that rewards you when you shop
  1. HSBC UK 
  2. Credit cards 
  3. Products 
  4. Rewards Credit Card

Rewards at the ready

With the HSBC Rewards Credit Card, you’re never far from a reward. Collect reward points by shopping with your card. Then use the Mastercard Pay with Rewards app or website to use your points towards purchases – online or in stores.

 

You need to have an HSBC Advance Bank Account (or have paid a total of £1750 a month into an HSBC UK Bank or Premier Account over the last 6 months).

Credit is subject to status, affordability and terms and conditions.

How to apply

Earn rewards you can access in an instant

Collect points

To collect reward points, simply shop as usual with your card. Get up to 10 points for every £1 you spend at selected retailers, and 1 point for every £5 you spend elsewhere. 100 points is worth £1.

Get bonuses

Get 2,500 welcome points worth £25 when you make your first transaction. Plus, spend £10,000 or more on your card in a year and we’ll give you another 2,500 bonus points.

Save money

Use your points towards purchases, wherever Mastercard’s accepted. There’s no waiting for vouchers or statements to arrive – use your points instantly with the Pay with Rewards app.

Collecting and spending reward points is easy. Find out how it works

Using your reward points

Card details

  • Pay with Rewards
    Use your reward points against everyday purchases, wherever Mastercard is accepted using the Pay with Rewards app or website.
  • 0% interest on purchases for 6 months* from account opening
  • 0% interest for 6 months* on balance transfers made within the first 60 days of account opening
    A 2% fee applies (minimum £5).
  • No annual fee
  • Apple Pay – immediate access to spending
    Add your new credit card to Apple Pay from the iOS version of the HSBC UK Mobile Banking app. This allows you to pay for things in shops, apps and on websites immediately using Apple Pay, while waiting for your new card to arrive.

* At the end of the promotional period, balance transfers and purchases will revert to the standard variable rate.

Using your card

  • Manage your card wherever you are
    You can use Online Banking or the HSBC Mobile Banking app to check your credit card balance, make payments and stay in control.
  • Get an additional card
    As long as they’re over 18, you can add your partner or a family member to your account.
  • Use your card abroad
    You can use your HSBC Credit Card at any retailer or cash machine that displays the logo on your card. Fees apply and non-HSBC cash machines may also charge a fee.
  • 24/7 worldwide assistance
    Help is always at hand if your card is lost or stolen. Wherever you are in the world you can speak to our dedicated team about getting an emergency cash advance.
  • Temporarily block your card
    For those times your card is misplaced, add a temporary block on your card, and simply unblock it when it turns up.

Apply for the HSBC Rewards Credit Card

Please ensure you read the important information in the ‘Things you should know’ section before applying.

Are you eligible? 

To be eligible for the HSBC Rewards Credit Card, you need to:

  • have an annual income/pension before tax of £6,800 or more
  • be a UK resident*
  • be over 18

And be an existing HSBC UK customer who holds one of the below:

  • an Advance account
  • a Bank Account or Premier Account, and have paid a total of £1750 a month into your account over the last 6 months, not including money transferred from other sole or joint personal accounts held with HSBC UK.

 

If you foresee any changes in circumstances that may affect your ability to repay the credit card you are applying for, for example a change in your income or outgoings, a change to your employment situation or you are a temporary or zero hours contract worker, please apply by telephone or branch.

 

Introductory rates only apply if you haven't held an HSBC credit card within the last 6 months. Balances can't be transferred from cards issued by other members of the HSBC Group including first direct, M&S Financial Services plc, John Lewis Financial Services Ltd.

 

If you’re not a HSBC customer yet but would like to take up the Rewards Credit Card today, you first need to successfully apply and qualify for an HSBC Advance Bank Account. To qualify for an HSBC Advance Bank Account you must pay at least £1,750 a month into the account (or a minimum of £10,500 every 6 months), not including money transferred from other sole or joint personal accounts held with HSBC UK.

 

Alternatively, you can apply for a HSBC Bank Account or HSBC Premier Bank Account and maintain a credit turnover of £1,750 a month into the account for a minimum of 6 months (or a minimum of £10,500 every 6 months), not including money transferred from other sole or joint personal accounts held with HSBC UK.

 

*Channel Islands and Isle of Man customers are not eligible to apply online. Please visit your local branch to apply.

Frequently asked questions

What is Pay with Rewards? 

Pay with Rewards is an innovative solution by Mastercard® that allows HSBC Rewards credit card customers to use their reward points against purchases they make at millions of participating locations worldwide, both for point of sale (POS) transactions as well as online transactions.

How does it work? 

Simply register via the Pay with Rewards mobile app, or through the Pay and Rewards website.

You’ll earn points whenever you use your card to make a purchase but you won’t be able to see or use your points until after you’ve registered.

You can redeem your points against a purchase at any time, even if you haven’t got enough points to cover the whole of a transaction. You can even set your preferences so your points are automatically used for your next purchase. The original purchase, and the corresponding Pay with Rewards credit, will appear on your monthly credit card statement.

And don’t worry, if you forget to allocate your points before making a purchase, you can still use them against a valid transaction made within the last 7 days on the App.

What are the benefits of using Pay with Rewards? 

With Pay with Rewards, you have endless opportunities for using points against purchases you make, in-store or online, at millions of eligible Mastercard locations, within and outside of the UK*. You can view and redeem your rewards via mobile app or online. Set your Pay with Rewards preferences and simply use your card like you normally would. The monetary equivalent of your points will be credited to your card account.

*(Any Non-sterling transactions will be charged with a Non-sterling transaction fee of 2.99%. The fees will be applied on the entire transaction amount regardless of the points used. Reward Points can’t be used against the Non-sterling transaction fee).

Will Pay with Rewards be available to other HSBC Credit Card holders?  

Currently, Pay with Rewards is only available to HSBC Rewards primary credit card holders.

Is there a fee to register or use Pay with Rewards? 

No, there is no charge associated for Pay with Rewards.
